## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow, transforming raw customer transaction records into actionable business insights.

The dataset was cleaned and prepared using Python, analyzed through SQL, and visualized using Power BI to uncover trends in customer behavior, sales performance, purchasing patterns, and operational efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Identify high-performing product categories
* Evaluate subscription-based customer engagement
* Analyze the impact of shipping methods on customer satisfaction
* Discover demographic trends influencing revenue generation
* Build interactive dashboards for business decision-making

## 📈 Key Insights Generated

* Clothing generated the highest revenue among product categories.
* Subscription customers accounted for a significant share of purchases.
* Younger and middle-aged customer groups contributed the highest sales volumes.
* Faster shipping options showed stronger customer satisfaction ratings.
* Digital payment methods such as PayPal and Credit Cards dominated transactions.
* Discount campaigns influenced purchasing behavior differently across age groups.
